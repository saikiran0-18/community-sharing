How It Works

1. Register

Create your account and choose whether you are a tool owner or borrower.

2. Request a Tool

Borrowers can post a request with tool name, dates, duration, and message.

3. Owner Accepts

Nearby owners can view the request and accept or reject it easily.

4. Verify & Pay

Users can verify details, complete payment, and borrow tools in a safer way.

Why Choose ToolShare?

Save Money

Borrow tools only when needed instead of buying them for one-time use.

Easy Access

Find useful tools from nearby people in your own area quickly and easily.

Secure Sharing

Verification and payment steps make the process more trusted and organized.

Community Help

ToolShare supports local sharing, reduces waste, and builds a helpful community.
